Pilot For A Day

‘Pilot for a Day’ events provide a unique opportunity for members to see what the whole fuss is about (and for catching the flying bug), as well as for pilots to build their flying hours in an affordable way by splitting cost with members they’re flying.

Schedule

PFD events are organized twice per semester in collaboration with local partner flight schools. The event takes place over a weekend with transportation to and from the airport provided by MSFC.

Cost

The minimum cost for being a ‘pilot for a day’ is around $65 per person. The actual cost can be less with special discounts applied depending on how many people register for an event. You will be paired up with an experienced pilot for your flight.
