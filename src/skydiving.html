Skydiving (SD) Division

The Skydiving Division manages tandem-jump events for beginners, and occasionally organizes indoor wind-tunnel exercises during winter.

About

The skydiving Division is dedicated to making it easier for McGill students to satisfy their inner thrill seeker, whether for the first or tenth time. The team helps set up transportation, group discounts and general logistics to both outdoor and indoor skydiving locations. Check out this video about indoor skydiving!

Cost

The costs associated with skydiving vary depending on number of people going and seasonal promotions. Indoor skydiving typically costs around $50-120 depending on the number of "jumps" and whether you have done it before. Outdoor skydiving costs around $200-260 depending on the number of people coming with the team. We strive to provide the cheapest transportation options possible but usually ask for $5-10 fee to help cover the cost.
