Skydiving (SD) Division

About

Our Skydiving Division is back and better than ever! Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or looking to conquer your fear of heights, this thrilling experience is a perfect opportunity to push your limits and create unforgettable memories.

How it works: Every year, we organize an exciting skydiving trip to Joliette, Quebec. Participants hop on a bus with fellow adventurers, head to Parachute Voltige – one of Quebec’s premier skydiving schools – and leap into the sky from a staggering altitude of 13,500 feet. It's a full day of camaraderie, excitement, and pure exhilaration.

Why should you sign up?: Skydiving is an experience like no other, and we make it accessible by offering a special discounted rate exclusively for our group. Typically, a jump costs $408.25, excluding transportation, but in 2024, tickets were priced at only $350, which included transportation, taxes, and the joy of sharing the adventure with a supportive group of like-minded thrill-seekers. This incredible offer ensures that anyone who signs up can enjoy this bucket-list activity at a fraction of the regular cost.

Where: The jump takes place at Parachute Voltige, a highly reputable skydiving school located in Joliette, Quebec. With experienced instructors, state-of-the-art equipment, and a track record of safety, they ensure that every participant feels confident and secure as they take the leap of a lifetime.

Don’t miss out on this adrenaline-pumping adventure. Spaces are limited, so grab your spot today and get ready for a breathtaking experience you'll never forget!

Cost

The costs associated with skydiving vary depending on number of people going and seasonal promotions. Indoor skydiving typically costs around $50-120 depending on the number of "jumps" and whether you have done it before. Outdoor skydiving costs around $320 depending on the number of people coming with the team. We strive to provide the cheapest transportation options possible but usually ask for $25-30 fee to help cover the cost.
