Flight Sim (FS) Division

MSFC can provide its Flight Sim gear: Yoke/Control Column, Throttle quadrant, Rudder pedals etc. for use within a Desktop Flight Simulation environment which can be especially useful with grasping the concepts of Instrument Flying. Pilots can recreate an entire Instrument approach in virtual reality before they fly it in real life! Let us know if you need Jepessen Sim charts (VOR, ILS etc.) for your airport. You can also use the Sim gear for fun even if you don't plan on using it as a tool for flight training.
