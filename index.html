McGill Students' Flying Club

In a short span, McGill Students' Flying Club has become one of Canadaâ€™s largest student-run aviation organizations. Operating from McGill University in Montreal, McGill Students' Flying Club provides a platform for its members to engage in flight training and other aviation-related activities. Our mission is to promote aviation and aerospace fields and foster a growing community of pilots and aviation enthusiasts.

Be part of a community that shares your passion!

Flight Training

In partnership with CPAQ.AERO, we offer Private Pilot Licence ground school right at McGill, on downtown campus. All McGill community can enjoy the ground school at a cost much lower than market price. The courses will prepare you for everything found on Transport Canada's exams.
Our partnership ensures a smooth experience for both aspiring and current pilots looking for flight training.

Opportunities

We provide countless opportunities for aviation enthusiasts of every level to take part in activities. From flying airplanes to jumping out of airplanes, we have something to offer everyone who is looking to explore their passion for aviation. Our team members can help you find a local flight school for a fun flight over the downtown area, or to help you on your way to a license.

A Community of Aviators

Beyond our activities and events, we are a place of assembly for all members of the aviation community. This is a place for aviation enthusiasts to meet industry professional; a place for aircraft mechanics to meet commercial pilots; a place to find others that share your passion.

Club Activities

Pilot for a Day (PFD)
(Operating normally)

The Pilot-for-A-Day event allows aspiring pilots to experience the wonders of flying for an affordable price. Even if you do not intend to pursue a pilot's license, a beautiful flight over downtown is a fun activity for all. In fact, we receive many requests from people wanting a surprise birthday gift for their partners!

If you have any questions, email us with [PFD] in the title.

Flight Simulator
(Operating normally)

Our simulator night events let pilots and non-pilots experience the power of PC flight simulations for fun and for training. It is the best time to socialize and meet others who share the same passion with you! And it is completely free! We operate both X-Plane 11 and Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 to satisfy every usage of PC flight simulations.

If you have any questions, email us with [Sim] in the title.
